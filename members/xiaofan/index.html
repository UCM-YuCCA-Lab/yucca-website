Research Interests

  • On-device AI for edge computing in real-world deployments
  • Embedded systems design for emerging applications and needs
  • Neuromorphic and next-generation edge intelligence

I am broadly interested in building intelligent and autonomous embedded systems. Much of my work involves collaborations with academia and industry partners (full collaboration list).
